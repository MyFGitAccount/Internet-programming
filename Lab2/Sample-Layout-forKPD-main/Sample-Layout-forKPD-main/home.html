Straight Line Motion

Definitions:

s: distance (m), t: time (s), v: velocity (m/s), va: average velocity

Formula: v = s t

Average velocity over multiple intervals:

va = (s₁ + s₂ + … + sₙ) (t₁ + t₂ + … + tₙ)
